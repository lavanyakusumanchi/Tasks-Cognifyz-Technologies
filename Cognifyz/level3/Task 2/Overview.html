Overview

Our one-month web developer internship at Cognifyz Technologies offers an immersive learning experience. You will work on real-world projects, enhance your coding skills, and receive mentorship from industry experts. We are excited to have you join our team and look forward to working with you.

After completing the first month, you will be required to go through an interview process. If you successfully pass, you will be eligible for a performance-based stipend ranging from 10,000 to 15,000 rupees, reflecting your dedication, skills, and performance.

At the end of the internship, you have the option to pay a registration fee of 249/- to cover administrative expenses for the interview process. This ensures a fair and structured evaluation. Alternatively, you can pay 149/- to obtain your Internship Completion Letter and Recommendation Letter without participating in the interview.
